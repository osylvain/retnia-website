Retnia is a Canadian company that provides innovative, non-invasive ocular oximetry tools to researchers and eye-care professionals.

Retnia has developed a patented, non-invasive device to accurately measure the concentration of oxygen in the human eye. This unique and revolutionary technology is the fruit of more than 15 years of research.

The technology enables researchers and eye-care professionals to gain insight into pathophysiological and metabolic processes. It offers applications for screening, diagnosing, monitoring, and managing the care of diseases such as glaucoma, diabetic retinopathy and age-related macular degeneration.onitoring, and managing the care of diseases such as glaucoma, diabetic retinopathy and age-related macular degeneration.

the idea

Using light to provide crucial information on the health of ocular tissues and processes involved in ocular diseases.

the technology

Quantitative, non invasive, continuous, real time, full light spectrum analysis for ocular oximetry.

the potential

Revolutionize the way we prevent, screen, diagnose and treat patients with ocular diseases.

how it works

Retnia’s technology is based on a patented instrument coupled with a unique software to accurately measure, non-invasively and in real-time, the concentration of oxygen in the human eye. Light emitted from the device travels through the eye, reflects on the eye fondus and returns. The light signal is treated and analyzed to provide an accurate, absolute measure of the concentration of oxygen in the eye. The measurements are then clearly presented in a user-friendly environment.

Retnia is a Canadian company whose objective is to provide innovative ocular oxymetry tools to researchers and ocular health professionals.

The unique technology is the fruit of more than 15 years of research. Retnia has developed a patented, non-invasive instrument to accurately measure the concentration of oxygen in the human eye.
